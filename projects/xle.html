shopify automation

we built a custom order form for a cycling apparel brand that enables individuals and teams to request fully custom-designed garments. customers can select products, upload logos, and input sizing through a personalised, streamlined experience. the system generates a quote and pushes it directly to shopify's backend, creating a structured workflow for follow-up and fulfilment.

a standout feature, "find your fit," guides users to the correct size based on their measurements—reducing uncertainty and improving order accuracy. this approach supports group orders and complex custom designs, delivering a premium, bespoke experience without relying on a traditional e-commerce cart.
